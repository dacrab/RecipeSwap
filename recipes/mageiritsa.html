Μαγειρίτσα της γιαγιας

Execution time Level of difficulty Quantity
30 Minutes Medium (5) 6 Plates

ingredients:

Half a kilo of lamb liver
1/2 Lemon
2-3 Rosemary Clips
3 T.S. Olive Oil
1 Onion
1 Garlic Cloves
¼ Bunch of Dill
7 Spring Onions
1,5 Liters of Water
Chicken Cube
αλάτι
Pepper
2 Lettuces



For the Egg Mixture:

2 Eggs, Medium
Lemon Zest , from 2 Lemons
Lemon Juice , from 2 Lemons
2 T.S Corn Flour



Execution Method

Place a pot over high heat, add water, salt and bay leaves, and bring to a boil.
Add the liver in the pot, squeeze the lemon and add the lemon rind.
Add the rosemary and boil for 15-20 minutes. Occasionally dip the liver with a spoon
Remove the pot from the heat, transfer the liver to a chopping board and finely chop it.
Place a second pot over high heat, add the olive oil and let it burn.
Finely chop the onion and garlic on a chopping board, add them to the pot, and sauté for 1-2 minutes.
Finely chop the dill stems, add them to the pot and set the leaves aside.
Finely chop the white part of the spring onions, add them to the pot, and keep the green part aside.
Add the liver, stir with a wooden spoon and sauté for 7-8 minutes, until nicely browned.
Add the water, bouillon cube, salt and pepper, and mix.
Lower the heat, cover the pot with the lid and simmer for 20-30 minutes.
Finely chop the lettuce, dill and the green part of the spring onions that we kept, and add them to the pot..



For the egg mixture:

In a bowl, add the eggs, lemon zest, lemon juice and cornstarch, and mix well with a hand whisk.
Place a sieve in the pot, submerge it and with a ladle take the filtered water, slowly pour it into the bowl and stir vigorously with a hand whisk. Pour a total of 5-6 tablespoons.
Pour all the egg lemon into the pot and mix with a spoon.
Remove the pot from the heat
Serve with olive oil and pepper.
