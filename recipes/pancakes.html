Pancakes In The Toaster

Execution Time difficulty Degree Quantity
10 Minutes Easy (3) 6-8 Plates

Συστατικά:

200 g. strained yoghurt
1 Egg, Medium
1 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, or 1 vanillin
90 ml Seed oil
Salt
50 g granulated sugar
120 g self-raising flour
1 teaspoon of baking soda

For serving:

Honey
Forest Fruits
Mint leaves
Powdered Sugar

Execution Method

In a bowl, add the yogurt, egg, vanilla extract, sugar, 50 ml of seed oil and salt and mix well with a spoon. The remaining 40 ml of seed oil will be used to coat the toaster.
Combine the flour and baking soda in a bowl, add them to the mixture, and stir gently for 10 seconds, just to mix the ingredients.
In a bowl, combine the flour and baking soda, add them to the mixture, and stir gently for 10 seconds, just to mix the ingredients.Place a toaster on the counter, plug it in, and let it warm up. If you have an option for a temperature setting, select medium.
Coat all 2 racks of the toaster oven with 1 teaspoon of seed oil using paper towels.
Transfer 1 tablespoon full of the mixture to the toaster oven, cover, and bake for 2-3 minutes.
Remove the pancake from the toaster, and repeat the same process for the rest of the mixture, brushing each time with 1 teaspoon of seed oil and the 2 grills..
Serve with honey and forest fruits, and optionally garnish with mint leaves and icing sugar.
