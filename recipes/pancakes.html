Pancakes In The Toaster

Prep: 10 Minutes Difficulty: Easy (3/10) Servings: 6-8

Ingredients

  • 200g Greek yogurt
  • 1 large free-range egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 90ml coconut oil
  • Pinch of Himalayan salt
  • 50g coconut sugar
  • 120g gluten-free self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder

For serving:

  • Organic maple syrup
  • Mixed berries
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Organic powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together Greek yogurt, egg, vanilla bean paste, coconut sugar, 50ml coconut oil, and Himalayan salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix gluten-free flour and baking powder. Gently fold into the wet mixture until just combined.
  3. Preheat your toaster oven to medium setting.
  4. Lightly brush both racks of the toaster oven with coconut oil using a silicone brush.
  5. Spoon 1 tablespoon of batter onto each rack. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. Carefully remove pancakes and repeat the process, brushing racks with oil between batches.
  7. Serve warm, drizzled with maple syrup, topped with mixed berries, fresh mint leaves, and a light dusting of organic powdered sugar.
